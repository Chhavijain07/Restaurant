About

JINENDRA KRIPA FAMILY RESTAURANT

Journey has been started by Mr. Purushottam Sharma in the year 1987 from the restaurant at sarvate bus stand Indore. With the determination, willingness, and hard work of GK family, today we are successfully running 5 branches at prime locations of Indore.


The leading vegetarian food chain in Central India, with promise of quality and taste is famous for its exotic spices, delicate herbs, and fine dining experience. We offer wide range in our menu that includes Punjabi, South Indian, North Indian, Chinese, Continental, Chaats and beverages. Foodies love some of our signature mouthwatering and lip-smacking delicacies.


Today we are the first choice of foodies among all the restaurants in Indore. We prepare our food fresh and with love as per the need of our customers. In order to maintain the finest quality and taste, best and authentic ingredients are delivered to our restaurants daily. Being the best restaurant in MP, Gurukripa Restaurant is committed to provide excellent food, high standard of service, and an unforgettable dining experience for you and your family to enjoy every day and many years to come.

  • To provide our customers happiness and joy with the food.
  • To serve delicious and amazing food & beverages.
  • To provide impeccable service to our customers consistently with warm greetings and efficiency to attend within 20 seconds.
  • To ensure easy reach for all food overs, with our strategic locations.

  • We are spreading our Aroma since last 33 years and have passionately served millions of customers.
